Ronald Reagan

The 40th president of the United States of America

Ronald Reagan in the Oval Office.

Here's a timeline of Reagan's life:
  • 1911 - Born in Tampico, Illinois
  • 1928 - Graduates from Dixon High School
  • 1928-1932 - Attends Eureka (Illinois) College, where he majored in economics and sociology
  • 1932 - Receives a temporary sports broadcasting job with WOC, a small radio station in Davenport, Iowa
  • 1937 - Enlists in the Army
  • 1940 - Marries actress Jane Wyman, whom he met while making the movie Brother Rat
  • 1941 - Daughter Maureen is born
  • 1945 - After the war, he resumes his acting career, which continues for 20 years
  • 1945 - Adopt Michael
  • 1949 - He and Wyman divorce
  • 1952 - Marries movie actress Nancy Davis
  • 1952 - Daughter Patricia (Patti) is born
  • 1962 - Officially changes his party registration to Republican
  • 1966 - Defeats incumbent California Gov. Edmund G. "Pat" Brown in a landslide
  • 1969 - Sends in the National Guard to break up protests at the University of California at Berkeley after university officials block activists' efforts to create a "Peoples Park"
  • 1980 - Elected president in a landslide victory over incumbent Jimmy Carter
